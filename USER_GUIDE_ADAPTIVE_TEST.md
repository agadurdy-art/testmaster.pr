## The Test

### Part 1: Reading (5-8 min)
- Read short passages
- Answer 3 questions
- Choose A, B, C, or D
- Questions get harder if you're doing well

**Example:**
```
Passage: "Sarah is a teacher. She works in London. 
She likes her job very much."

Question: What is Sarah's job?
A) Student
B) Doctor
C) Teacher ✓
D) Engineer
```

### Part 2: Speaking (5-8 min)
- Answer 3 questions by voice
- Click "Start Recording"
- Speak for 30-60 seconds
- Click "Stop Recording"

**Example Questions:**
- Tell me about yourself
- What do you do in your free time?
- Describe a place you like

**Tips:**
- Speak clearly
- Take your time
- Don't worry about small mistakes

### Part 3: Writing (5-8 min)
- Write a short paragraph
- Minimum 30 words (aim for 50-100)
- Topic: About your daily routine

**Example:**
```
I wake up at 7 AM every day. I have breakfast 
with my family. Then I go to work by bus. I work 
in an office from 9 AM to 5 PM. In the evening, 
I like to watch TV and read books.
```

## Tips for Best Results

### Before the Test
- ✅ Take it when you're fresh (morning is best)
- ✅ Find a quiet place
- ✅ Use good headphones/microphone
- ✅ Allow 25-30 minutes
- ❌ Don't rush - accuracy matters more than speed

### During the Test
- ✅ Read questions carefully
- ✅ Take your time
- ✅ Speak clearly for speaking section
- ✅ Write naturally (don't try to impress)
- ❌ Don't leave answers blank

### After the Test
- ✅ Review your mistakes carefully
- ✅ Understand WHY you got them wrong
- ✅ Start recommended courses
- ✅ Practice daily
- ✅ Retake test in 1-2 months to track progress

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I take the test multiple times?**
A: Yes! Take it every 4-8 weeks to track your progress.

**Q: How accurate is the band score?**
A: Very accurate! Our AI examiner uses official IELTS criteria. Your score should be within ±0.5 of an official test.

**Q: What if I don't have a microphone?**
A: You can skip the speaking section, but your overall score will be less accurate.

**Q: Can I pause the test?**
A: Yes, there's no timer. You can take breaks between sections.

**Q: Are the courses free?**
A: Foundation courses (for beginners) are FREE. Other courses are $29-79/month.

**Q: What's the difference from the Quick Level Test?**
A: The Adaptive Test:
- ✅ More accurate (Band 2.0-9.0 vs 4.0-9.0)
- ✅ Shows specific errors
- ✅ Gives learning path
- ✅ Takes longer (20 min vs 10 min)

**Q: Can I see my old results?**
A: Yes! They're saved in your dashboard under "Recent Tests".
